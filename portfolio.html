Naruto
You must watch it!

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. Throughout the series, Naruto faces various challenges and battles as he grows stronger and forms bonds with his friends and allies.
